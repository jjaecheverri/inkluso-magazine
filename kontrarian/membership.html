The Strategy

Why we're asking you to wait.

Content first.

We publish before we charge. Read us first. See if we're worth believing in. Pay only if you do. Trust isn't bought — it's built one piece at a time.

One voice per city.

The trust comes from knowing who wrote it and why. One Kontributor per city. No committee. No brand voice. Just a real person who actually lives there.

No algorithm.

Membership unlocks a curated feed, not a recommendation engine. We decide what's worth reading. You decide if you agree. That's the deal.
