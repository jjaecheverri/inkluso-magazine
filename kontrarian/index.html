What we are

A different kind of
cultural intelligence.

Most content about cities is written from the outside looking in. We've never been interested in that view.

Ground-level truth

Every piece is written by someone who lives in that city — pays rent there, knows the streets, feels the shifts. Not a journalist parachuted in for a weekend. Not an algorithm predicting what you want. The real thing.

The Kontrarian Scale

Every piece is scored for depth, authenticity, and cultural weight. Not stars. Not likes. A deliberate evaluation of whether something earns its place in the conversation — or just fills space.
