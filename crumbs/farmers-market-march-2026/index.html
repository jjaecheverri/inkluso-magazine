Farmers Market Cluster

Minimum publishable signal units tracking structural movement in U.S. farmers market infrastructure, vendor economics, and governance. March 2026 data pack.

GROUND · Farmers Market 12 Crumbs March 3, 2026
FM-C-001 GROUND NWA HIGH

Bentonville FM Adds Wednesday Market

$3M+ in 2025 sales triggered temporal expansion. Downtown Bentonville Inc. launches weekday market May 6–Sept 30 on A Street Promenade. 25 vendors expressed interest before deadline. The Saturday-only model is no longer enough.

MECHANISM: revenue-threshold → infrastructure-expansion → temporal-diversification
Source: NWA Democrat-Gazette, Feb 9, 2026 · Tier B
FM-C-002 GROUND Rogers, AR HIGH

Whole Foods Opens 2nd NWA Store

39,500 sq ft in Rogers with 120+ local items. Dustin Kennedy — official title: "forager" — sources NWA producers for Amazon's shelf. Ozark Natural Breads, Onyx Coffee Lab now dual-channel. The supply chain just got a corporate landlord.

MECHANISM: corporate-entry → local-supply-absorption → dual-channel-pricing
Source: Progressive Grocer, Feb 18, 2026 · Tier B
FM-C-003 GROUND Duluth, MN HIGH

Duluth Vendors Self-Organize After Nonprofit Collapse

Community Action Duluth canceled Lincoln Park market in Oct 2025. Vendors unpaid since June. State investigation ongoing. Vendors launched Twin Ports Roots Market bi-weekly in same food desert neighborhood. Governance failure became structural repair.

MECHANISM: governance-failure → vendor-displacement → self-organization → infrastructure-repair
Source: Northern News Now, Feb 6, 2026 · Tier B
FM-C-004 GROUND National HIGH

15,000 U.S. Farms Disappeared in 2025

Total now 1.865 million — lowest in seven years. 8% decline since 2018. Texas saw the largest drop. Average farm size keeps climbing. The producers who supply farmers markets are disappearing faster than the markets are growing.

MECHANISM: consolidation-pressure → small-farm-exit → supply-chain-thinning
Source: USDA, reported by Farmers' Advance, Feb 17, 2026 · Tier A
FM-C-005 GROUND National HIGH

Farm Bankruptcies Surge 46% in 2025

American Farm Bureau reports the sharpest annual increase in bankruptcies since the pandemic. Midwest filings up 70%. Net cash farm income revised down $27B below projections. The economic floor beneath small producers is cracking.

MECHANISM: income-decline → debt-compression → producer-exit
Source: American Farm Bureau / DTN Progressive Farmer, Feb 18, 2026 · Tier B
FM-C-006 GROUND National MODERATE

Sprouts Plans 40+ Stores Using "Farmers Market" as Brand

So Sprouts Farmers Market — which is a supermarket chain, not a farmers market — is opening 40+ new locations in 2026 after $8.8B in FY2025 revenue. The name says "farmers market." The logistics say Amazon's third cousin. Cool. Now explain who the farmer is.

MECHANISM: brand-appropriation → consumer-confusion → category-dilution
Source: Seeking Alpha / Business Wire, Feb 2026 · Tier B
FM-C-007 GROUND National MODERATE

SNAP Cuts Hit 2.4 Million Per Month

CBO estimates SNAP participation drops by 2.4 million people per average month over 2025–2034. Many farmers markets accept EBT/SNAP — this is a direct demand signal. Fewer benefit dollars flowing through market terminals means less vendor revenue in food-access zones.

MECHANISM: policy-restriction → demand-contraction → vendor-revenue-loss
Source: Congressional Budget Office / Civil Eats, Feb 2026 · Tier A/B
FM-C-008 GROUND National MODERATE

USDA Cancels $1B+ Local Food Purchasing Program

Secretary Rollins canceled LFPA and Local Food for Schools early — eliminating over $1 billion that farms expected. The Farm Bill draft proposes a $200M replacement with no mandatory funding. "An authorization of appropriations is essentially an empty promise." — NSAC

MECHANISM: federal-defunding → institutional-demand-collapse → small-farm-revenue-gap
Source: Civil Eats, Feb 17, 2026 · Tier B
FM-C-009 GROUND St. Johnsbury, VT MODERATE

Vermont Adds Wednesday Market Too

Caledonia Farmers Market Association launches new Wednesday market in St. Johnsbury starting June 10, 2026 at Four Seasons Park. Same temporal expansion pattern as Bentonville. The Wednesday market isn't a local experiment — it's a structural trend.

MECHANISM: demand-saturation → temporal-expansion → weekday-infrastructure
Source: North Star Monthly, 2026 · Tier B
FM-C-010 GROUND NWA MODERATE

NWA Retail Vacancy Hits 3%

Northwest Arkansas retail vacancy at approximately 3% in early 2026 — virtually no available space. Whole Foods didn't find an opening, it created demand for one. When retail space is this tight, every new food store reshapes the local food economy.

MECHANISM: vacancy-compression → competitive-displacement → capital-reallocation
Source: Arkansas Business, 2026 · Tier B
FM-C-011 GROUND National MODERATE

Online SNAP Shopping Becomes Permanent

Farm Bill draft mandates USDA make online SNAP shopping permanent — no longer a pilot. Plus chip-enabled EBT cards within 6 months to prevent skimming. Digital payment infrastructure at farmers markets just got a federal mandate.

MECHANISM: policy-mandate → digital-payment-infrastructure → market-access-expansion
Source: Civil Eats / Farm Bill draft, Feb 17, 2026 · Tier A/B
FM-C-012 GROUND National LOW

Food Prices Projected to Rise 3.1% in 2026

USDA forecasts 3.1% food price increase for 2026 with beef leading the climb. When grocery prices rise, farmers market price premiums compress — the gap between "local artisan" and "Walmart shelf" narrows. That changes who shops where.

MECHANISM: price-inflation → premium-compression → behavioral-migration
Source: USDA Food Price Outlook / RFD-TV, 2026 · Tier A
